Admissions

Transparent, Merit-Based and Structured Admission Process

Admission Overview

Admissions are conducted strictly as per government norms and institutional guidelines. Selection is merit-based and ensures transparency, fairness and equal opportunity to all eligible candidates.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Admission primarily at Class VI level.
  • Student must meet prescribed age criteria.
  • Must have completed previous academic level from a recognized institution.
  • Reservation policy applicable as per government rules.

Selection Procedure

Selection is conducted through an entrance examination designed to assess mental ability, scholastic aptitude and language proficiency.

Admission Process

Step 1: Notification of Admission published on official website and notice board.
Step 2: Submission of online/offline application form within stipulated time.
Step 3: Conduct of Entrance Examination.
Step 4: Declaration of Merit List.
Step 5: Document Verification and Medical Examination.
Step 6: Final Admission Confirmation.

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous Class Marksheet
  • Transfer Certificate
  • Caste/Category Certificate (if applicable)
  • Residence Certificate
  • Passport Size Photographs

Residential Facility

The institution is fully residential. Selected students are provided hostel accommodation, dining facilities, uniforms and academic support as per institutional guidelines.

Fee Structure

Education and boarding facilities are provided as per government funding norms. Nominal charges may apply as per official policy.

Important Note

Admission is strictly based on merit and compliance with eligibility norms. Any form of recommendation or external influence will not be entertained.
